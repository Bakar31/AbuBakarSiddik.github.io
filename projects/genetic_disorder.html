Early Prediction of Genetic Disorder

  • Tech Stack: Python, Scikit-Learn, Logistic Regression, Catboost, GredientBoost, LGBM, Random Forest, Feature Engineering, Feature Selection
  • Github: Project Link

Goal: The goal of this project was to develop a classifier that can Genetic Disorder as well as Disorder Subclass at very early stage.

About Data: The dataset contains medical information of children who have genetic disorders.

  • train.csv: 22083 x 45
  • test.csv: 9465 x 43

    • My Approch: The dataset was super messy. I needed to lots of preprocessing to make it usable. feature engineeing and feature selection was done to boost the score. I applied multiple algorithms like Logistic Regression, Catboost, GredientBoost, LGBM, Random Forest.

    Best Score:

  • Genetic Disorder Class: 0.606
  • Disorder Subclass Class:0.4197
